DftFunctionals.change_parameters â€” Method

Return a new version of the passed functional with its parameters adjusted. This may not be a copy in case no changes are done to its internal parameters. To get the tuple of adjustable parameters and their current values check out parameters. It is not checked that the correct parameters are passed.
